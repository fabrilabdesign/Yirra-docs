# Arms Assembly

:::caution Safety First
Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment when working with adhesives. Ensure proper ventilation and follow all manufacturer safety guidelines for adhesives and solvents.
:::

## Adhesive Preparation

Proper arm assembly requires **both bonding surfaces** to be thoroughly coated with structural adhesive. The adhesive creates a permanent, load-bearing bond between the arm tube and mounting bosses.

### Recommended Adhesive

**3M Scotch-Weld DP490**

**Why DP490?**
- High-strength structural adhesive
- Excellent gap-filling properties
- Resistant to environmental conditions
- Approved for demanding applications

## Surface Preparation

1. **Clean all surfaces** thoroughly with isopropyl alcohol to remove oils and contaminants
2. **Ensure surfaces are dry** before applying adhesive
3. **Apply adhesive to both surfaces** for maximum bond strength
4. **Work quickly** - adhesives have limited working time

## Adhesive Application

**Critical:** Apply adhesive to **both surfaces** - the inside of the arm boss and the end of the arm tube.

## Assembly Process

### Step 1: Prepare Components
- Clean all bonding surfaces with isopropyl alcohol
- Ensure components are at room temperature (68-72째F / 20-22째C)

### Step 2: Apply Adhesive
- Apply a uniform bead of DP490 to both surfaces
- Use approximately 1-2 grams per joint
- Work adhesive into surface for maximum contact

### Step 3: Assemble Immediately
- Insert arm tube into mounting boss
- Align components precisely
- Apply firm pressure to ensure full contact
- Wipe away any excess adhesive

### Step 4: Cure Period
- Allow 24 hours at room temperature for full cure
- Do not disturb assembly during cure time
- Maintain temperature between 60-80째F (15-27째C)

## Quality Verification

:::tip Acceptance Criteria
- **No gaps visible** around the joint perimeter
- **Excess adhesive** properly cleaned from exterior surfaces
- **Components aligned** without twist or offset
- **Firm resistance** when testing joint integrity
:::

## Common Issues

### Insufficient Adhesive
**Symptom:** Gap visible around joint
**Solution:** Disassemble, clean surfaces, reapply adhesive to both surfaces

### Misalignment
**Symptom:** Arm does not sit flush in mounting boss
**Solution:** Check for debris, ensure proper cleaning of surfaces

### Premature Movement
**Symptom:** Components shift during cure period
**Solution:** Use clamps or fixtures to maintain position during cure
